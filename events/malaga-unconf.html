DETAILS

Our successful experiment in Berlin goes big(er) at ElixirConf EU. The organization will be similar, with some improvements.

Unconference

Prior to the date, a topic submission window will open for participants. The submissions will be visible from the time they are submitted, so everyone can see what's already there. There will be three formats:

  • Teach
  • Discuss
  • Learn

A Learn topic is a call for experts to Teach what they know. Since the submissions will be public as they are posted, this gives experts a chance to prepare.

Nerves Workshop

Co-located with the Unconference (in the same room as of now), the traditional conference Nerves satellite event is here. The Nerves core team loves a good pre-conference gathering to get into the groove. Meet a smaller group of devs, hack on stuff, talk, share ideas.

There will be a one or more Talk slots reserved for Nerves as part of the Unconference, the rest will be hands-on.

We hope to show never-before-touched hardware. We'll bring, embedded hardware, half-baked software and an issue tracker full of hopes and dreams. Hardware will be provided to experiment with. Together we build up the Nerves Starter Kit. Elixir skills are strongly recommended but not strictly required. Bring your computer and lets get hacking. Expect an informal workshop and hack session environment.

Rumor has it that some people might get to bring hardware home. More details to come (check back here).
