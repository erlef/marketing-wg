FAQ

Can I submit separately for myself, my company, my project?

Yes.

You can support as yourself, for your company, for your project. You can submit separately for each, or together (the form supports this). If you are sure for yourself, but will need to sort out the others, submit for yourself now and return for the others.

Where will this go?

It will be included with the documents submitted for the grant.

How will this be published?

There will be a webpage (to be announced) that will list all the submitters that approved (by checking the boxes on the form). It will include names, companies, projects, and even logos submitted. If you are not ok with the publicity, indicate so on the form.
